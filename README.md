# DPRK_ITW_financial_behaviour_analysis
I conducted an extensive on-chain behaviour analysis of Ethereum wallets publicly attributed to DPRK Information technology Workers. My goal was to explore beyond existing narrative understanding of the threat and examine their financial behaviour. Especially, how they receive earnings, how they move funds, how do they coordinate payouts and whether their activities align with well-known DPRK revenue generation tradecraft.
Wallet Address include: Publicly attributed addresses listed in the June 2025 DOJ Civil Forfeiture filing
